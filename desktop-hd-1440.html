What we do!
We are Global Garden and we aim to be an international produce market which places emphasis on health and sustainability. We encourage those in our vicinity to come experience what we offer and did we mention? Our produce is completely free!
Greenhouse
Robots

The robots are a great way to deliver different produce to members of the community without having to leave their residences. Together with Kiwibot, members of the community can use their mobile devices to place orders and meet the small, semi-autonomous robots whenever their order is delivered to their destination or whenever is most convenient.

This will allow people to grow to produce year-round and avoid weather challenges. The idea is that it serves as a  community garden and everyone in the community contributes to it that wants to benefit from it because the only rule is that for everything that they take, they must plant something new. They can take as much as they want as well.

Produce

We provide free fresh produce 24/7 to people in different communities around the nation. This is for people who may not be able to afford grocery store products or want produce that does not have pesticides or that isn’t genetically modified.
